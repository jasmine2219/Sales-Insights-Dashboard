## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Sales Insights Dashboard developed using Power BI and MySQL. The dashboard transforms raw transactional sales data into actionable business insights, enabling stakeholders to monitor revenue performance, evaluate market contribution, and identify high-value customers and products.

## Problem Statement

The organization generates large volumes of sales transaction data across multiple markets, customers, and products. However, the absence of a centralized reporting system makes it difficult to track performance trends, identify growth opportunities, and support data-driven decision-making.

## Business Problem

Business stakeholders needed a solution to:

* Monitor overall revenue performance.
* Compare revenue contribution across different markets.
* Identify top-performing customers and products.
* Track sales trends over time.
* Access key business metrics through a single interactive dashboard.

## Dashboard Features

* Revenue Analysis
* Sales Quantity Analysis
* Revenue by Markets
* Sales Quantity by Markets
* Revenue Trend Analysis
* Top 5 Customers
* Top 5 Products
* Year-wise Filtering

## Key Insights

* Delhi NCR contributes the highest revenue among all markets.
* Revenue generation is concentrated within a limited number of customers.
* A small group of products contributes significantly to total sales revenue.
* Revenue distribution varies considerably across different markets.
* Time-based analysis enables monitoring of revenue fluctuations and sales trends.

## Analytical Approach

To address the business problem, raw sales data stored in MySQL was integrated with Power BI for analysis and visualization. Data relationships were established across transaction, customer, product, market, and date dimensions to create a structured analytical model.

Using SQL-based data extraction, Power Query transformations, and DAX calculations, the dashboard was designed to provide a consolidated view of business performance. Interactive visualizations were implemented to enable market-level comparison, customer contribution analysis, product performance evaluation, and trend monitoring.

This analytical solution converts raw transactional data into meaningful business intelligence, supporting faster and more informed decision-making through data-driven insights.
